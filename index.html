KIM NAMJOON

Kim Nam-joon, known professionally as RM, is a South Korean rapper, songwriter, and record producer. He is the leader of South Korean boy band BTS. RM released his first solo mixtape, RM, in 2015, followed by his second mixtape, Mono, in 2018. Later in 2022, he released his Solo debut Studio Album called Indigo which was a hit. The album peaked at number three on the Billboard 200, becoming the highest-charting album by a Korean soloist of all-time.
